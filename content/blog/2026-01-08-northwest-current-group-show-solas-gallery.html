I am part of Northwest Current, a group exhibition at Solas Gallery in Seattle. The show is a survey of recent work from artists based in or connected to the Pacific Northwest, running from January 8 through February 20, 2026.

The exhibition features work by 21 artists: Aaron Morton, amanda j mead, Andrew Roibal, Danielle Zhang, Emmett Orgass, Eva Sköld Westerlind, Frederick Fehr, Isaac McKenna, Jack Johnston, Joan Dinkelspiel, Jon Feinstein, José María Salazar Jr., Joshua H Phelps, Kevin Junker, Lauren Max, Liza Qun Zhang, Lola Reinhardt & Petra Lavin, Marcellus Bonow-Manier, Marcus Shriver, Nick Thompson, Quinlan Corbett, Rachel Demy, and Sierra Ayers.

Solas Gallery is located at 300 South Washington Street, Unit Z, Seattle, WA 98104. Open Saturdays 1–5pm, First Thursdays, or by appointment.
